OUR STORY

The story of Edge has been one long in the making where we as two men met during a spell working together and from that became close friends. It was always an ambition for both of us to have our own business, either alone or with another within our area of expertise. Following on from a few long discussions and in line with what we felt was an opening in the market where we could also add value, we decided that it would be to in both ours and our future clients benefits to come together in form a fit-out company. With our partnership we feel we can bring together an offering that captures the needs of our clients through all stages of the fit-out process. From the design and its materials, to the commercial understanding of best market rate with the technical coordination and procedure to completion. All resulting in a more productive and enjoyable journey for our clients.

MEET THE TEAM

The Team at Edge have been mindfully and carefully selected to ensure that the characteristics of both partners and our work ethic is radiating within the office and our projects. The technical team here at Edge is small but greatly efficient which offers the benefits of being streamline in communication and focused on generating a more personal interaction and experience for our clients throughout the project journey.
